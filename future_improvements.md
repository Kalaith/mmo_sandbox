# Future Improvements - MMO Sandbox

## Single-Player World Systems
1. **Persistent World Simulation**: Complex world state with AI kingdoms, economies, and evolving storylines
2. **Dynamic World Events**: Random world events affecting economics, politics, and available quests
3. **NPC-Driven Economy**: AI-controlled market dynamics with supply and demand affecting item values
4. **Territory Management**: Control and develop regions with construction rights and resource management
5. **Multi-Region Gameplay**: Connect different world areas for large-scale adventures and trading

## Advanced Character Progression
6. **Multi-Class Systems**: Hybrid character builds combining multiple skill trees and abilities
7. **NPC Mentorship**: AI mentors providing training and guidance with mechanical progression benefits
8. **Character Reputation**: Fame and infamy systems affecting NPC interactions and available quests
9. **Skill Mastery Rewards**: Legendary abilities and titles unlocked through dedicated skill progression
10. **Character Legacy**: Create multiple characters with shared family history and inherited bonuses

## AI Guild & Social Simulation
11. **AI Guild Systems**: Compete with AI-controlled guilds in strategic battles and resource competition
12. **NPC Alliance Networks**: Form alliances with AI factions for collaborative projects and wars
13. **Guild Progression**: Unlock advanced facilities, exclusive content, and unique customization options
14. **Social Event Simulation**: Participate in AI-organized events with mechanical rewards and storylines
15. **Communication Systems**: Interact with AI characters through dialogue trees and relationship building

## Content Creation Tools
16. **Player Housing**: Extensive construction system with functional furniture and customization options
17. **Quest Design Tools**: Create custom quests with reward systems and export functionality
18. **Dungeon Builder**: Design custom dungeons with monster placement, loot tables, and difficulty scaling
19. **Modular Content System**: Extensible framework for custom content integration and sharing
20. **Event Scripting Tools**: Create dynamic scenarios, spawn encounters, and develop custom storylines
